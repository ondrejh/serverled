Typical set of RS-274X file names:

Copper:
  Gerber TOP layer                  *.GTL
  Gerber inner plane 1 (TOP)        *.GP1
  Gerber inner plane 2 (BOTTOM)     *.GP2
  Gerber BOTTOM layer               *.GBL

Silk screen:
  Gerber TOP ovelay                 *.GTO
  Gerber BOTTOM overlay             *.GBO

Solder stop mask:
  Gerber TOP solder stop mask       *.GTS
  Gerber BOTTOM solder stop mask    *.GBS

Soldering paste:
  Gerber TOP paste                  *.GTP
  Gerber BOTTOM paste               *.GBP

Mechanical dimensions:
  Gerber mechanical 1               *.GM1

Drilling:
  Excellon drilling                 *.DRD
